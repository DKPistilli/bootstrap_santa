A Chill Dude

Workin Hard or Hardly Workin?

Gavin? He's doing both. Known for his dedication, Gavin once suggested to a group of friends that "we should all get jobs" at the tender age of thirteen. Despite his burning passion for jobs, he's also been known to watch 7 hours straight of The Walking Dead and play through the entire Gears of War Campaign in his friends attic.
